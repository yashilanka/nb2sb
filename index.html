Nb2Sb - Navbar2Sidebar

Nb2Sb - Navbar2Sidebar is a Bootstrap-only jQuery plug-in that converts your navbar into a sidebar.

Bootstrap & jQuery

It works perfectly with Bootstrap 3. You won't need more apis and more code, you just have to include it in your website and the plug-in will do everything you need. Since Bootstrap 3 comes along jQuery you will need to include the jQuery library too, and I suggest to also include the jQuery-UI library.

Get Bootstrap 3 and jQuery

Try it yourself

Open this website with a smart-phone or shrink your browser's window, the menu will disappear just as you were visiting any Bootstrap-built website, but as you tap or click on the menu-button, a sidebar will appear.
